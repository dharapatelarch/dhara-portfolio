About

I AM AN ARCHITECTURE STUDENT DRIVEN BY A CURIOSITY FOR SPACES SHAPING HUMAN EXPERIENCES. I AM INTO DESIGNING ENVIRONMENTS THAT FEEL INTUITIVE, COMFORTABLE, AND PURPOSEFUL— WHERE FUNCTIONALITY MEETS EMOTION AND EVERYDAY USE BECOMES MEANINGFUL. DURING MY ACADEMIC JOURNEY, I HAVE WORKED ON A RANGE OF PROJECTS INCLUDING INSTITUTIONAL, RESIDENTIAL, AND LANDSCAPE PROJECTS, DEVELOPING A VERSATILE UNDERSTANDING OF DESIGN ACROSS SCALES AND CONTEXTS. I ASPIRE TO BUILD A PROFESSIONAL PATH IN THE FIELD BY CRAFTING HUMAN-CENTERED SPACES THAT QUIETLY ENHANCE DAILY LIFE THROUGH WELL-CONSIDERED DESIGN.
